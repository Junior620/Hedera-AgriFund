Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our platform

Unlike traditional microfinance, we leverage blockchain technology to create a transparent, global marketplace. Our platform tokenizes real agricultural assets, enabling instant liquidity and reducing intermediary costs by up to 70%.

Your assets are secured through Hedera's enterprise-grade blockchain technology, multi-signature smart contracts, and third-party audited security protocols. All tokenized assets are backed by verified physical collateral stored in certified warehouses.

We support major commodity crops including coffee, maize, rice, wheat, cocoa, and soybeans. Our platform integrates with certified storage facilities and quality assessors to ensure accurate valuation and secure storage of your agricultural assets.

Once your assets are verified and tokenized, loan matching typically occurs within 2-24 hours. Fund disbursement happens instantly through stablecoin transfers once loan terms are agreed upon by both parties.
